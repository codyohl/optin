Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Cody Ohlsen ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Game Camera Notifications service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our SMS notification service for game cameras.

2. Information We Collect

When you opt in to our service, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Camera Information: Camera ID and associated settings
  • Usage Data: Information about when and how you use our service
  • Communication Data: Records of your consent and opt-in/opt-out requests

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you SMS notifications about your game camera activity
  • Provide and maintain our notification service
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations and telecommunications regulations
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Maintain records of your consent and preferences

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on:

  • Consent: You have explicitly consented to receive SMS notifications
  • Legitimate Interests: To operate and improve our service
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with telecommunications regulations including TCPA and CTIA guidelines

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With telecommunications providers to deliver SMS messages
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale, or transfer of business assets

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our service and comply with legal obligations. When you opt out of our service, we will retain minimal information as required by telecommunications regulations, typically for a period of at least 4 years.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Opt-Out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe from notifications
  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

9. SMS/Text Messaging Specific Terms

Regarding our SMS notification service:

  • Message frequency varies based on camera activity
  • Message and data rates may apply from your carrier
  • We use toll-free numbers to deliver messages in compliance with CTIA guidelines
  • Opt-out is available at any time by texting STOP
  • For help, text HELP or contact us directly

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

11. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

12. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to request deletion of your information.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated effective date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

14. Telecommunications Compliance

Our service complies with:

  • Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA)
  • CTIA Messaging Principles and Best Practices
  • Toll-Free Number verification requirements
  • FCC regulations regarding SMS communications
